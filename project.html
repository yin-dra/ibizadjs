About us

DJ Lovespread - NL

DJ LoveSpread has come to develop his own unique, diverse sound by drawing hypnotizing, deep and funky tracks out of many different styles, produced with attention and love. More than ten years ago in his hometown of Rotterdam, Matan became part of the forward thinking Triphouse Rotterdam movement and has come to make dance floors burn in places like; Amsterdam, Berlin, Ibiza, London, Melbourne, Marrakech... Today DJ LoveSpread is busy playing records in his DJ residencies, organising parties and working in his studio creating his own trademark sound.

DJ Will Fly - SP

DJ Will Fly was literally born on the dancefloor. As a Hip Hop dancer, he was dipped into funky, afro and groovy soups which shapped his flow. His jams come from the very roots of movement. He started playing in international dance battles and ended up brewing his own juicy electronic style. Will was born in Spain but has african roots that have moved him around the globe, shaping a unique open-style that will serve every dancefloor.
